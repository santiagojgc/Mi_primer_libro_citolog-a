# Introduction

Welcome to **My First Cytology Book**.

This educational resource is designed to serve as an interactive guide for studying and analyzing cytopathologies, providing a dynamic learning experience for health sciences and biology students.

## Featured Content

In this interactive book, you will have access to practical, visually enriched content:

* **[Image Analysis of Breast Cytologies](06_breast_cytology/image_analysis.md)**: An interactive sample chapter detailing Fine Needle Aspiration Cytology (FNAC) evaluation, correlation of malignancy criteria, and the decision-making algorithm based on the Yokohama System.

```{note}
This interactive book incorporates dynamic diagrams, comparative tables, and self-assessment tools to enhance the practical learning process.
```
