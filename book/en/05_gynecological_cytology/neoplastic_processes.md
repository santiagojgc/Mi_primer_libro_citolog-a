# Unit 5: Analysis of Cervicovaginal Smears in Neoplastic Processes

This chapter covers the evaluation and classification criteria for cervicovaginal intraepithelial lesions and associated carcinomas, focusing on Bethesda nomenclature, low-grade (L-SIL) and high-grade (H-SIL) lesions, squamous cell carcinoma, and glandular abnormalities.
