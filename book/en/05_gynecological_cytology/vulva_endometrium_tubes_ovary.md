# Unit 7: Analysis of Cytological Samples of Vulva, Endometrium, Tubes, and Ovary

*(Translation pending / Traducción pendiente)*

This chapter covers the cytological and histological evaluation of non-cervical gynecological organs, including vulvar lesions (infections, VIN, squamous cell carcinoma), endometrial cycles, endometritis, endometrial hyperplasia, endometrial adenocarcinoma, fallopian tubes, and ovarian cysts and tumors.
